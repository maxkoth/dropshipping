# SaunaZen — Order Fulfillment SOP

**Platform:** Shopify + DSers | **Supplier:** CJ Dropshipping | **Updated:** June 2026

---

## DAILY ROUTINE

### Step 1 — Order Review & Fraud Check
- [ ] Shopify Admin → Orders → Unfulfilled (last 24 hours)
- [ ] Review fraud scores — cancel HIGH risk orders immediately, hold MEDIUM risk pending email verification
- [ ] Flag international orders: verify shipping zone compatibility (CJ Express covers US, CA, UK, AU, most EU)
- [ ] Note: larger/heavier items (2.8kg) occasionally trigger additional customs review for EU shipments

### Step 2 — Push to DSers
- [ ] Select all verified orders → "Order to CJ Dropshipping"
- [ ] Confirm all show "Ordered" within 10 minutes
- [ ] CJ typically ships within 48–72 hours; express method targets 7–12 day delivery

### Step 3 — Tracking & Communication
- [ ] DSers imports tracking automatically — verify orders from 4+ days ago have tracking
- [ ] WISMO emails: respond within 4 hours with tracking link + expected delivery window
- [ ] Proactively email customers if tracking shows no movement after 5 days

### Step 4 — Returns & Damage Claims
- [ ] Approve return requests within 24 hours — no questions asked
- [ ] Use domestic return address (do NOT route returns to China)
- [ ] Submit CJ damage disputes within 10 days of delivery with photo evidence
- [ ] Issue refund immediately on customer return delivery confirmation

---

## WEEKLY

- [ ] Check FIR blanket inventory levels on CJ — alert at < 80 units
- [ ] Monitor delivery time averages — escalate if avg exceeds 14 days
- [ ] Review ad performance vs. ROAS targets (goal: > 3.0x)
- [ ] Check competitor pricing on AliExpress for benchmarking

## MONTHLY

- [ ] Reconcile supplier invoices vs. Shopify revenue
- [ ] Run pricing-calculator.js — verify margins remain above 25% net
- [ ] Refresh UGC content — request before/after photos from happy customers
- [ ] Test new ad creative; retire underperforming sets

---

## ISSUE RESOLUTION

| Issue | Action | Timeline |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Defective heating zone | Replace immediately + CJ dispute | 24-hour replacement offer |
| Customs held (EU) | Proactive email to customer, provide customs code | Same day |
| Non-delivery claim | Reship + CJ claim | 48 hours |
| EMF safety question | Direct to product certification page | 4 hours |
